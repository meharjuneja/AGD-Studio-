OUR SERVICES

At Aditi Gupta Designs, we craft spaces that blend style, functionality, and individuality. With expertise in interior, exterior, and landscape design, we bring each client's vision to life.

Aditi Gupta Designs offers tailored design services that transform interiors, exteriors, and landscapes into inspiring spaces. We focus on quality, detail, and sustainability, shaping environments that are both timeless and uniquely yours.

ICONIC ARCHITECTURE

Design is a manifetation of a story. Tailor a space to the versatile tastes of discerning clients, Principal Architect, Aditi Gupta begins by assimilating a narrative that spans both functional and aspiratonal preferences in mordern as well as classic style signatures. Beginning with intricate sketches and swatches that cpture the client's desired outcome, the team creates conceptual study models and highl sophisticated renders to visualize the process and results. From preliminary conception through design evoluton, the team works inventively to deliver unique spaces with a signature style.

INTERIOR DESIGN

Discerning spaces deserves bespoke interiors Aditi Gupta combines visual dexteruty with an unparalleled access to internationally renowned materials, craftmenship and processes that result in striking and exceptional homes. Her heightened sense of proportion creates elegant and timeless interiors of both contemporary and classically inspired homes. Throughout the process, the team includes sketches and elaborates mood board presentaions, including the clients in the creation process.

FURNITURE

Commissioning everything from concept, design and build, the Houses of Aditi Gupta creates luxuriously unique furniture that echoes the overall architechture and aesthetic palette of high-caliber process. Her keen eye lends itself to the selection or design of each indvidual piece of furniturein every space of the home including entrance foyers, lounges, bedrooms, formal rooms and pool areas. Both customized and sourced pieces of furniture meet the studio's sophisticated standards of quality and follow a cohesive aesthetic that proportionally focused as welll as visually striking.

SMART HOME

Whether enhancing an existing space or designing anew, the House of Aditi Gupta takes a forward-thinking approach to tech-integrated living. Our team balances functionality, cost-effectiveness, and user-friendliness, crafting smart spaces that cater to a diverse range of preferences and needs. Each detail is thoughtfully integrated, ensuring a seamless and intuitive experience that adapts to modern lifestyles, creating homes that are not only efficient but effortlessly connected and tailored to individual desires.

DECOR

From accents to accessories, the finishing touches highlight the soul of a space. We have an exclusive team of consultants who assist in commissioning or sourcing bespoke pieces, including crystal chandeliers, precious display items, antiques, and limited-edition pieces. Each selection enhances the elegance of the interiors, imbuing each room with character and timeless appeal, ensuring every element aligns with the client's vision and personality, and brings a distinct charm to their home.

ARTISTRY

The House of Aditi Gupta sources art work that reflects the narrative of a space as well as curate its placementin accordance with the architechture, interiors and lightings. Leveragin a formal association with reputed art galleries, we engage in creating an art collection that extends the narrative of the home and its residents. The curatorials process includes vetting the right medium or style and commisioning original artwork from both emerging and established artists. The studio offers consultation and coveted access to international art channels to our most discerning clients.

LANDSCAPE DESIGNS

With a focus on harmonizing indoor and outdoor spaces, the House of Aditi Gupta creates landscape designs that are both geologically and architecturally synchronized. Our projects feature unique greenscapes, including lush tropical rainforests with serene water bodies and manicured gardens adorned with classical fountains. We also curate outdoor artworks and design elements, ensuring each landscape reflects the beauty of nature while enhancing the overall aesthetic of the property. Each design is a testament to the seamless integration of environment and architecture, creating inviting and inspiring outdoor spaces.

LIGHTINGS

Lighting in many ways, is the nervous system of the home. Conjoing lighting design with architectural features and interior concepts is crucial to creating a synergetic space. Aditi Gupta applies her fusion of classically symmetric, yet experimentally bold, style to design exceptional customized chandeliers, which are manufactured in high-caliber materials by world-renowned studios. We align conception and execution with liighting design and placement to seamlessly amplify design elements.

TRUNKEY EXECUTION

Uniquely positioned to meet comprehensive demands from ideation and concepts to construction and decor, the House of Aditi Gupta is a one-stop studio. Our turnkey business model ensures that our clients receive articulate and cohesive results, better value for time, and exceptional attention to detail. Our turnkey services seamlessly bridge the gap between conception, design, and final build, providing a smooth and efficient process that prioritizes client satisfaction. With a dedicated team overseeing every aspect, we guarantee a finished product that exceeds expectations and embodies the vision of each client.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Aditi Gupta Designs provide for residential and commercial spaces? +
At Aditi Gupta Designs, we offer a range of services, including interior design, exterior enhancements, and landscape planning for both residential and commercial spaces. Our team works closely with clients to bring their vision to life, ensuring a cohesive and personalized design that enhances functionality and aesthetics.
How do you determine the budget and timeline for a design project? +
We start by discussing the project scope, size, and style preferences with you. Our Budget Calculator provides an initial cost estimate, and the Project Timeline Estimator gives you an idea of how long the project might take based on your requirements. These tools offer a solid foundation, which we further refine during in-person consultations.
Can you help with choosing color schemes and materials? +
Absolutely! We understand that selecting the right colors and materials is essential for any project. Our Color Palette tool helps narrow down options based on your preferences, while our designers guide you in choosing high-quality materials that align with your style, budget, and overall vision for the space.
How involved can I be in the design process? +
We encourage client involvement at every stage of the design process! Whether it's taking our Design Style Quiz to clarify your style, reviewing design drafts, or selecting materials, we value your input. Our collaborative approach ensures the final design truly reflects your tastes and lifestyle.
Can I make changes during the project? +
Yes, we allow for changes during the project. However, changes may affect the timeline and budget.
