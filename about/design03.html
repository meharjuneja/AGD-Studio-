ABOUT US

PASSION AND PURPOSE

FOUNDER's STORY

Aditi Gupta is a talented interior designer and the visionary behind her eponymous design studio. Born and raised in the vibrant city of Mumbai, Aditi's passion for design blossomed from a young age, influenced by the rich culture and stunning architecture that surrounded her. Her dedication to her craft led her to pursue a degree in Interior Architecture from the prestigious University of the Arts London (UAL), where she immersed herself in the world of design.

During her time at UAL, Aditi gained a comprehensive education in various aspects of design, including space planning, material selection, and sustainable practices. She had the opportunity to collaborate on diverse projects, ranging from chic urban apartments to innovative commercial spaces. This experience allowed her to refine her unique aesthetic and develop a design philosophy centered around functionality and beauty.

Aditi's education not only equipped her with technical skills but also fostered a deep appreciation for global design trends and cultural nuances. She draws inspiration from her travels, exploring different architectural styles and design practices around the world. Her ability to blend contemporary elements with traditional influences results in spaces that are both innovative and timeless.

Beyond her design expertise, Aditi is committed to sustainability and believes in the power of thoughtful design to enhance the quality of life. She actively seeks out eco-friendly materials and practices, ensuring that her projects not only look beautiful but also contribute positively to the environment.

With a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating personalized spaces, Aditi strives to transform her clients' visions into reality. She believes that every space should tell a story, reflecting the individuality and lifestyle of its inhabitants. Her dedication to excellence and her ability to connect with clients make her a sought-after designer in the industry, and she continues to push the boundaries of creativity and innovation in her work.

Founded by Aditi Gupta, a passionate interior designer with an eye for detail and a flair for innovation, the brand has grown from a one-person dream into a sought-after name known for its creative excellence.

OUR APPROACH

At Aditi Gupta Designs, our approach is grounded in the belief that every interior should be a true reflection of the client's unique needs and lifestyle. We craft bespoke, thoughtfully designed spaces that harmoniously blend aesthetics with functionality, creating homes that are as liveable as they are beautiful.

With a deep understanding that success is defined not only by the outcome but by the client's experience, we take on a select number of projects each year. This ensures each client receives the highest level of personalised attention and service. From the first meeting to the final reveal, we are committed to delivering exceptional results, ensuring every space feels elegant, comfortable, and distinctly tailored to its owner

Our Philosophy

Designing with Purpose, Crafting with Passion

At Aditi Gupta Designs, every creation is more than just aesthetics; it's a thoughtful blend of creativity, functionality, and individuality. Our philosophy centers on bringing out the unique story of each client, ensuring that every design isn't just visually captivating but also deeply resonant. By combining innovation with timeless elegance, we strive to craft pieces that stand out and stand the test of time.
