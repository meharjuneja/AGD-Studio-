ABOUT ADITI and THE TEAM

Aditi Gupta Designs is a Mumbai-based design studio specializing in luxury residential interiors and exteriors across India and internationally for both private clients and developers.

Led by Aditi, our experienced team of talented Interior & Architectural Designers provides a personalized design service to create detail-driven, luxury spaces that perfectly suit their context, meet client needs, and stand the test of time.
