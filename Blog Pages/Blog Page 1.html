Patio Revamp; Before and After

I can't believe it's been almost four years since we moved into our new home! One of the projects that have been on our bucket list is to revamp our patio area. We worked really hard on it and I'm so excited to finally share the completed project with you guys!

Patio Revamp BEFORE

Patio Revamp AFTER

Such a big difference from how it looked before lol… We love it so much! This area has become our new sanctuary during the warmer weather. It kind of feels like a combination of a cabana and another addition of a family room that we get to enjoy comfortably.

As you can see, we also installed artificial turf over the reddish colored deck. We really enjoy how the turf feels when we walk on it. It almost feels like carpet, soft, and comfortable. The turf also gives this area a cleaner and polished look and couldn't be happier with how everything turned out!

More Pictures!!

Of course, I'm not going to forget to talk about one of the main decor pieces of our patio revamp! We are completely in LOVE with this beautiful outdoor seating!! It is very simple, elegant and so classy. It sits deep and it's perfect for us because my family and I are all tall lol!
