Subtle and Soft Thanksgiving Tablescape

This year for my color scheme, I decided to create a very subtle and soft Thanksgiving tablescape with muted colors like greys, creams and a touch of blush….hope you like it!

Although I admire all the colors of Fall for Thanksgiving, I tend to gravitate towards a more subtle and softer color palette. For me, I find that you can always “add more” when you already have subtle colors going in your decor. It could be anything from adding a pop of color or adding more decor pieces.

One of the things I always enjoy incorporating in my designs is having beautiful throws and pillows! It just ties everything together nicely and makes the space look even more cozier! In this photo, I decided to use a soft and subtle grey throw and tie it with a gold, beaded pumpkin pillow. I've also linked similar sources for you below!
