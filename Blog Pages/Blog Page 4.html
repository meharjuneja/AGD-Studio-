HomeGoods Fall Home Tour

This year, I have really enjoyed changing things up & decorating for Fall. I don't know if I was ready for a change of weather {because its was too warm for too long in California!} Or I was just ready for a new look…regardless, I love changes!

Before I begin any further, I'm excited to share that The Bay Area Home ladies & I are doing a Fall Home Tour sponsored by HomeGoods.

When I originally started shopping for Fall decorations, particularly gold pillows….I really didn't have any luck at first because I was looking for a particular shade of gold. One day, I went to my local HomeGoods with my mom…& it's always nice to go shopping with moms because sometimes they spot the best treasures for you. Like when I was shopping for gold pillows!

Fall means creating an ambiance that is warm, cozy & inviting. The thought of spending days where you just turn on the fireplace, cuddle and watch a movie with your family…with hot chocolate of coarse
