Tis The Season to Decorate

Tis the season to decorate! Hi Friends and welcome to my Christmas home tour where I'm joining some of my wonderful blogger friends who are also sharing their homes for the holidays.

This year, I decided to add some gold and silver tones to my holiday decor. I actually decided to add the plaid Christmas ribbon last minute and really love how it turned out…hope you like it too ♥

The month of December is just so pretty! Nevertheless, December is also one of the busiest months for us. Not only do we celebrate Christmas but we get to spend three birthdays, and a wedding anniversary together; five occasions throughout the month!! It's so crazy yet SO much fun so when I start to decorate the house for Christmas; it stays pretty festive for all the occasions we get to celebrate…it's a win win situation, cant complain. Tis the season to decorate' everyone!!

I love taking out our Christmas dishes every year. I remember all those little laughs and seeing joys in my kids eyes whenever we shared delicious meals together. I would make them their favorite drink; hot coco with marsh mellows. Gathering around the table, I remember the kids feeling extra special eating from these dishes knowing we get to open Christmas gifts right after…it was so sweet! Ah those precious little memories..I would do anything to go back in time and hold them tight when they were so little ♥ Oh and I decided to make hearts tied with ribbons out of these candy canes
